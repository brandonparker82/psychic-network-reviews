Our Full Review

Purple Garden Overview

Purple Garden is a newer entrant that has quickly gained popularity with its modern, app-first approach to psychic readings. The platform stands out with video reading capabilities and a sleek user experience.

Video Readings

Purple Garden’s standout feature is video readings. Being able to see your advisor creates a more personal and trustworthy experience compared to text or phone-only platforms.

User Experience

The app is beautifully designed with intuitive navigation. Advisor profiles are visually rich with ratings, specialties, and clear pricing. The overall experience feels modern and polished.

Our Verdict

Purple Garden is perfect for users who want a modern, visual reading experience. The video feature adds a level of connection that text-based platforms can’t match. Low starting rates make it accessible for newcomers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Purple Garden mobile-only?

The app experience is clearly the primary one and has most of the advisor roster, but the web version now supports chat and video readings too. On desktop you miss the push-notification onboarding and the Apple Pay convenience.

How does the $10 signup credit work?

Create an account, add any payment method, and Purple Garden drops a $10 credit on top of your first deposit. It must be used within 30 days and cannot be withdrawn.

Can I do video readings?

Yes — video is a headline feature on Purple Garden, unlike Kasamba or Keen where video is rare. Expect to pay an extra ~$1/min premium on top of the chat rate for the same advisor.

Is my data private?

Readings are not retained for download by advisors. Chat transcripts are stored server-side for abuse moderation per the privacy policy; you can request deletion of your account and associated transcripts via support.

How strict is advisor vetting?

Stricter than Keen, looser than California Psychics. Applicants do a live video audition and must maintain a 4.5+ customer rating in their first 90 days or be delisted.
